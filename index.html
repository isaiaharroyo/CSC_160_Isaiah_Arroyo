Music

Of the many things that has continuously evolved throughout my life, from the effervescence and inexperience of youth, to the maturity and stability of adulthood has been my library of music.

I grew up with a more conservative taste in music, limiting myself to a set number of artists and genres. It wasn't until High School that I found that the best way to enjoy the experience of music is by exploring new artists and sounds that I would have previously thought of as bizzare or odd.
